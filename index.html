Why We Run This Campaign

#AirSwapChallenge

We are launching this campaign because we believe everyone deserves the right to breathe clean, healthy air.

Vaping is not just a personal habit — it impacts young people, public health, and community well-being. Through this campaign, we aim to raise awareness about the hidden dangers of vaping and encourage healthier, smoke-free lifestyles.

In South Korea, youth e-cigarette use has sharply increased, with recent studies showing around 7% of high school students reporting recent use and nearly 30% using both e-cigarettes and traditional cigarettes. Misconceptions about reduced harm, attractive device designs, and social influence make young people especially vulnerable. We want to correct these misconceptions and protect their health and future.

Together, we can make a difference by choosing fresh air over harmful vapors.
